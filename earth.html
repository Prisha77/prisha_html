EARTH

You and I believe I was made by the almighty,
And all he asked in return was to keep me neat and tidy.
I remember the days I used to be all green,
But you all ruined me by not keeping me clean.

I have seen a million people throw garbage on me,
The rest million ignore even after all the garbage they see.
I have seen slogans of Clean Earth,
Yet no one waters me to quench my thirst.

I have provided you with everything you need,
The least you could do is plant a seed.
I have seen all kinds of pollution- water, land, air ,
All I can say is treat me with care.

I have lived with all kinds of animals and human being,
And over years dirt is all I am seeing .
Your decision about going to mars I know,
Now nobody cares about me what I'll do, Where I'll go?
