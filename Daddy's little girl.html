Daddy's little girl

I know you feel those old days are gone,
When you used to sit beside me and tell stories till dawn.
Teaching me alphabet and playing old games,
But now for you only the memory remains.

I know you miss carrying me around on your shoulder,
Wishing I did not get any older.
You think your daddy's little girl has aged,
But how to tell you 'I never changed'.

I know you still fear I will leave you,
But you can trust me that will never be true.
You sometimes wish again I were a child,
Miss the way I talked and smiled.

I know you think I am mature and grown,
Thinking your daddy's little girl is now unknown.
And there is on.y one thing I can say,
Your daddy's little girl didn't fade away.
