Sis

When I am crying upset day/ night,
She is the one sitting by my side.
Not letting me lose any hope,
Making me smile by her silly joke.

More than sister she is my best friend,
With whom most of the time I spend.
She is unique and special,
With two sides of her sweet and rebel.

I know if the world is against me by my side she will stand,
Being the only one to lend out her hand.
She is my best friend, sister and guide,
In front of her I got nothing to hide.
