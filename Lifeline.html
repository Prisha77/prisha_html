Lifeline

They could enjoy a luxurious dinner with their family,
But they chose to make you live happily.
Risking their life to save someone else's,
Daily they are buzy checking peoples heartbeat and pulses.

Whenever we are sick we know we can count on them,
They are definitely our life gem.
They know they have got a huge responsibility,
Busy all day yet give us 24/7 facility.

How much pressure they must feel when someone's life is in their hand,
Performing prosedures that only they understand.
Daily thousands of people come to them with various sickness,
And they help them as family and not like a part of business.

They have once spent their leisure time just to help you,
They care about their patients true.
Imagine how much they have studied to come this far,
They are our universe's true star.
