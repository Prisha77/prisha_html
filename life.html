Life

It all begins with you in a cradle
With no worries of your future being dull,
All around you ready to play
Making you sleep night and day.

Just in one blink you are in your teens
Getting to know what life really means,
Now you know you are starting to change
Having all mood swings are a little strange.

Now you are a youth with real responsibility
Where getting a job depends on your capability,
Money is what you run after
And dear ones now don’t matter.

Now you are getting old
Realizing your past was gold,
Never gonna get the loved ones back
Now you know in life happiness is what u lack.
